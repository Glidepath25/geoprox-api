How GeoProx Works

  1. Drop a pin, type coordinates, or draw your works area.
  2. Optionally add the permit number for tracking and audit.
  3. GeoProx runs live proximity searches against trusted datasets.
  4. Generate permit-ready PDF reports in seconds for distribution.
  5. Capture site assessments and sampling records that sync desktop ↔ mobile.

Everything is stored with an audit trail so operational teams, QSs, and permit offices stay aligned.

Key Benefits

  • Automated compliance.
    Removes the manual admin burden of desktop searches and document prep.
  • Permit-ready output.
    Produce maps, risk ratings, and PDFs that satisfy Street Works requirements.
  • Search history.

  • Full auditability.

  • Mobile ↔ desktop sync.

  • Collaboration built-in.
